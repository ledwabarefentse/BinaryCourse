Conversion from Decimal to Binary

To convert any decimal number D to binary, follow these steps:

  1. Divide D by 2.
  2. Record the remainder from the division.
  3. Record the result rounded down to the nearest whole number.
  4. Update D with the rounded-down value.
  5. Repeat these steps until the division result is 0.
  6. Arrange all recorded remainders in reverse order to get the binary representation.

Worked Example:

Let D = 15:

  • 15 / 2 = 7, Remainder = 1
  • 7 / 2 = 3, Remainder = 1
  • 3 / 2 = 1, Remainder = 1
  • 1 / 2 = 0, Remainder = 1

The binary representation of 15 is thus 1111.
